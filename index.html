Why Buy Luneko?

Wholly Peer-to-Peer (P2P)

LUNE is fully peer-to-peer and tradeable via DEX (decentralized exchange). Each user of LUNE is a co-owner of the Luneko ecosystem.

Distribution Rewards

As transaction volume increases, the 2% transaction value redistribution accumulates holdings within your wallet.

There are a lot of Luneko.

There are 18,400,000 to be precise! The relatively  low number is both an homage to a long-time cryptocurrency project and a means to allow the project to have some breathing room. With a conservative and capped supply, opportunities for growth are orders of magnitude more present.

$LUNE does not include a team presale or dev fund: Its progress is beholden solely to community involvement. Due to prohibitively expensive gas fees at the time of launch, the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) was chosen as the network backbone for this token over Ethereum.

How to buy $LUNE

Step 1: Install Metamask for Chrome or another BSC-compatible browser wallet.

This includes wallets like the official Binance browser wallet and Mathwallet! If you already have these, you're good to go!

Send BNB to Metamask

Send BNB to your new Metamask wallet from your existing wallet (e.g. Trust Wallet or Binance).

Connect your Metamask wallet to PancakeSwap

Our launching point is PancakeSwap! It's a decentralized exchange for purchasing and trading BSC tokens. Visit PancakeSwap and insert the token contract into the exchange search bar: 0xAcD1cAeF47E4c47BafE8a51B3F4305Fc38203b7A

Exchange your $BNB for $LUNE

You're ready to go! Set your slippage (optimally around 3%) and swap the amount of BNB you'd like for your intended amount of $LUNE. Welcome aboard!
