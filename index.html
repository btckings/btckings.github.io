InuYasha Incubator

USE CASE

The InuYasha ecosystem will exist as a decentralized vetting and donation program for promising new tokens. New teams will submit their soon to launch Protocol, Blockchain Game, Meme Token, or any project, charity, org that wishes to apply for a donation from our community.

  • Application by potential project – Decentralized vetting by InuYasha holders
  • Donation recipient gives InuYasha holders pre sale access
  • Winner receives a boost in donation and marketing by the InuYasha community
  • InuYasha holders will get the earliest access to community vetted and 100% safe projects.

Disclaimer: InuYasha is designed to be a community generated prize and decentralized blockchain political statement. InuYasha community contributors should not expect any reward or return for contributing to the InuYasha donation ecosystem. That being said… To the Moon!
